# FlowTrack

## Overview

FlowTrack is a SaaS supply chain management platform designed for SMEs.

It enables organizations to manage:

- Purchase Orders
- Supplier Payments
- Multi-Warehouse Inventory
- Shipment Tracking
- Logistics Analytics

## Problem

Many SMEs rely on fragmented spreadsheets to manage procurement and inventory.

This results in:

• Poor visibility  
• Payment tracking issues  
• Inventory mismatches  
• Shipment delays  

## Solution

FlowTrack provides a unified cloud platform that delivers:

- Real-time inventory visibility
- Automated procurement workflows
- Shipment monitoring
- AI-powered demand forecasting

## Target Market

SMEs in:

- Logistics
- Retail
- Manufacturing
- Import/export
